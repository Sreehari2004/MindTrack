About MindTrack

Our Mission

MindTrack is dedicated to helping individuals understand and manage their stress levels effectively. We believe that everyone deserves access to personalized stress management solutions that work for their unique situation.

Our Approach

Through our scientifically-backed assessment of 20 carefully crafted questions, we evaluate your stress levels and provide tailored recommendations. Our scale of 0-2 helps you clearly understand where you stand and what steps to take next.

Why Choose MindTrack?

Our platform combines psychological expertise with practical solutions, offering you a comprehensive approach to stress management. We focus on providing actionable advice that you can implement in your daily life.
